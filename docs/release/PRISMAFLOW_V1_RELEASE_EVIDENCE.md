## Known Limitations
1. **Three starter pages only**: The app ships with Prism Fox, Calm Mandala, and Space Whale. No user upload, marketplace, or AI generation features are included (per design scope lock).
2. **Blind Guide Mode TTS depends on browser support**: `speechSynthesis` is gated behind a `'speechSynthesis' in window` check; unsupported browsers silently skip TTS.
3. **Brush canvas fixed at 1000×1000 logical pixels**: Brush strokes may scale differently on very high-DPI screens; this is acceptable for v1.0.
